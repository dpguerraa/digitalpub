The Story of Bernardo Carpio

   

Bernardo Carpio is a heroic figure in Tagalog folklore, sometimes seen as a giant with superhuman strength. In some versions, he’s trapped between two mountains by Spanish colonizers but remains alive, holding the mountains apart.

   

His legend is often interpreted as a symbol of resilience and resistance.
